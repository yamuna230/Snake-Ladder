About the Game

🎯 Snake & Ladder is a fun-filled, turn-based board game that originated in ancient India. It symbolizes life's journey—full of ups and downs!

🎲 How to Play

  • Each player takes turns rolling the dice.
  • Move forward based on the dice number.
  • If you land on a ladder, climb it.
  • If you land on a snake, slide down.

🧠 Strategy Tips

  • Play smart: watch your positions closely!
  • Don’t celebrate too early—snakes can surprise you.
  • Climb every ladder you can!
📜 Fun Fact: Originally called "Moksha Patam," the game was designed to teach morals—ladders represented virtues and snakes represented vices.
